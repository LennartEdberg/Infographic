Wife Carrying Contest
Objective

The objective is for the male to carry the female through a special obstacle track in the fastest time.

 History

The sport was first introduced at Sonkajärvi in Finland, the championships have been held annually in Sonkajärvi since 1992.

 Rules

The length of the official track is 253.5 meters. The minimum weight of the wife to be carried is 49 kilograms. All participants must enjoy themselves.

Countries

Wife carrying is now practiced in Australia, the United States of America, Hong Kong, Estonia,UK.

 Championships

2013 – Taisto Miettinen and Kristiina Haapanen (Finland).
2012 – Taisto Miettinen and Kristiina Haapanen (Finland).
2011 – Taisto Miettinen and Kristiina Haapanen (Finland).

Prize

The prize depends on the wife's weight in beer.
